India’s premier manufacturer of plywood, block boards and flush doors, is synonymous with exceptional quality of products and unparalleled service to its distributors, dealers and customers alike. Over the decades later, we havw came to represent a trusted brand with immaculate product integrity and market goodwill. Today, the company has a rock-solid foundation of state-of-the-art factories and a devoted nationwide network of offices, distributors, dealers and channel partners.

Our goal is to remain at the forefront of innovation and continue to bring revolutionary products to the burgeoning market of new India. We hope to bridge the gap between quality and affordability, and offer economical products without compromising on durability and workmanship. Furthermore, we understand our responsibility as stakeholders in the conservation of our ecological resources and are investing in updating our machinery and training our personnel to convert our manufacturing units into zero-waste facilities. We are also infusing resources into R&D of even more sustainable, alternative building material for the future.
